Arthroploid

This game utilizes ES2015 features, and requires at least Firefox 21, Chrome 45, or Microsoft Edge. Not tested on Safari and not supported on IE.

Instructions

Move around the lower portion of the screen with mouse hover. Click down on left-mouse button to shoot. Watch out for collisions! Colliding with an enemy means instant death, and you have only so many lives Every 4th round, you will have to face a daunting boss. Be careful, as they may spawn more enmies with each section destroyed.

Due to real life circumstances, this game stands incomplete as of the time of submission.
