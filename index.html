Beat-'em-All!

Christopher Philabaum Source (GitHub)

This game utilizes ES2015 features, and requires at least Firefox 21, Chrome 45, or Microsoft Edge. Not tested on Safari and not supported on IE.

Instructions

Move left and right with the A and D keys respectively. Use spacebar to throw punches! Press R to reset. Press Enter to pause the game. Use Esc to go back to the main menu. Press M to toggle / (un)mute the title screen music and background music. Watch out, as the further you progress, the more enemies you have fend off. How many can you take down before you go down?
